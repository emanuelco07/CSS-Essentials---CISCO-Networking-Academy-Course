The Great Wall of China Isn’t Visible from Space

Despite the popular myth, astronauts have confirmed that the Great Wall cannot be seen from space with the naked eye. Still, it remains one of humanity’s most iconic engineering achievements.

The Milky Way Contains Over 100 Billion Stars

Our galaxy is a massive spiral structure filled with stars, dust, and dark matter. Even traveling at the speed of light, it would take about 100,000 years to cross it.

Deep-Sea Creatures Can Produce Their Own Light

Many organisms living in the ocean’s darkest depths use bioluminescence to hunt, hide, or communicate. In places where sunlight never reaches, glowing becomes a survival strategy.
